Eclipse

$600,000,000

The Eclipse is one of the largest and most luxurious yachts in the world, owned by Russian billionaire Roman Abramovich. It boasts numerous extravagant features, including multiple swimming pools, a cinema, and a helicopter pad. It’s also equipped with a missile defense system.

Azzam

$600,000,000

Azzam holds the title of the largest private yacht in the world, measuring 180 meters (590 feet). Owned by a member of the royal family of the UAE, Azzam offers unmatched opulence, including luxurious interiors, a full-service spa, and advanced technological features.

Dilbar

$800,000,000

Dilbar, owned by Russian billionaire Alisher Usmanov, is one of the world’s largest yachts, with a gross tonnage of over 15,000 GT. The yacht has an extensive range of luxurious amenities, including a 25-meter pool, a spa, and a hangar for the owner’s helicopters.

Queen Mary 2

$460,000,000

The Queen Mary 2 is one of the most famous luxury liners in the world. It’s known for its opulent interior, iconic ocean cruises, and transatlantic crossings. The ship features lavish staterooms, a planetarium, a grand ballroom, and a swimming pool.

Symphony of the Seas

$1,350,000,000

Symphony of the Seas is one of the largest cruise ships in the world, operated by Royal Caribbean. It features an array of entertainment options, including a water park, theaters, ice-skating rink, and multiple restaurants. The ship can carry up to 6,680 passengers.

Norwegian Bliss

$1,000,000,000

Norwegian Bliss is a premium cruise ship operated by Norwegian Cruise Line. It features a stunning observation lounge for scenic cruising, a two-level electric go-kart track, laser tag arena, Broadway-style entertainment, and a luxurious spa. It’s designed for both adventure and relaxation.

Emma Maersk

$145,000,000

Emma Maersk is one of the largest container ships in the world, operated by Maersk Line. It’s part of the Maersk Triple-E class and is known for its massive size and carrying capacity. It can carry up to 18,000 containers, making it a key player in global trade.

OOCL Hong Kong

$160,000,000

OOCL Hong Kong was once the largest container ship in the world, owned by the Hong Kong-based shipping company Orient Overseas Container Line (OOCL). It can carry up to 21,000 TEU (twenty-foot equivalent units), setting new standards for container shipping.

MSC Gülsün

$140,000,000

MSC Gülsün is a massive container ship operated by Mediterranean Shipping Company (MSC). It is one of the largest container ships in the world and features cutting-edge technology to reduce fuel consumption and emissions, making it more eco-friendly.
