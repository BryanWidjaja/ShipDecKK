About Us

Anchored in legacy, driven by innovation — discover who we are.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK quickly established itself as a trusted name in the maritime industry, recognized for its unparalleled customer service and top-quality vessels. From luxury leisure yachts to robust commercial shipping solutions, ShipDecKK offers a diverse fleet tailored to meet the evolving needs of its clientele.

Committed to excellence, the company uses only the finest materials and partners with skilled craftsmen to ensure that every vessel upholds the highest standards of design and durability. With a strong reputation for reliability and customer satisfaction, ShipDecKK continues to set the benchmark for quality in modern shipbuilding.

Our Mission

To connect the world to the maritime industry through a dynamic digital platform. We aim to inform, inspire, and immerse users in the wonder of ships—from luxury yachts to industrial giants. Our platform bridges curiosity and knowledge, making the maritime world more accessible to everyone.

Our Values

Legacy & Heritage

With over two decades of history, we honor the past while embracing the future of maritime innovation.

Striving for Excellence

We provide high-quality visuals, accurate information, and smooth user experience that set us apart.

Integrity & Trust

Transparency, respect for maritime facts, and ethical presentation are at our core.

Continuous Innovation

From static galleries to interactive ship explorers and AI recommendations, we evolve constantly.

Community & Collaboration

We support a vibrant network of maritime enthusiasts, historians, professionals, and curious newcomers.

Sustainability & Responsibility

We promote eco-friendly maritime practices and raise awareness about preserving our oceans for future generations.
