Our Story

Founded in 2001, ShipDecKK began as a niche platform dedicated to cataloging and showcasing the finest ships in the world. What started as a small-scale website run by a team of maritime enthusiasts has evolved into a globally recognized destination for ship lovers, industry professionals, and collectors alike.

Over the past two decades, we’ve expanded from simple ship listings to a comprehensive digital gallery offering rich media, ship specifications, historical insights, and immersive experiences. From our first hand-coded pages to today’s dynamic, interactive platform, we’ve continuously adapted to meet the changing tides of technology and user expectations.
