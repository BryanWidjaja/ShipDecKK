Frequently Asked Questions

What is ShipDecKK?

ShipDecKK is a premium ship provider known for top-quality vessels and exceptional customer service. Founded in 2020, the company offers a wide variety of ships—from luxury yachts to commercial vessels—crafted with the finest materials by expert shipbuilders.

What types of ships do you offer?

ShipDecKK offers a diverse fleet including leisure yachts, cargo ships, fishing boats, commercial vessels, and custom builds tailored to specific client needs.

Can I customize a ship to my preferences?

Absolutely. ShipDecKK works closely with clients to design and build custom ships that meet specific requirements in style, performance, and features.

Where are your ships built?

Our ships are built in specialized shipyards using only premium materials. We collaborate with expert craftsmen and naval architects to ensure each vessel meets our exacting standards.

Do you offer international shipping or delivery?

Yes, ShipDecKK provides international delivery and logistics support, ensuring your vessel arrives safely and efficiently anywhere in the world.

What kind of warranty or support do you provide?

All our ships come with comprehensive warranties and post-sale support, including maintenance packages, service assistance, and a dedicated customer care team.
